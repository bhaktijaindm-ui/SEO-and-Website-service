Scribe: Accelerating to 131,000 Visitors

Deep Dive · B2B SaaS · Technical SEO

0 to 131K Monthly Visitors in Under a Year

Solving exactly 100% of the user's specific problem for instant conversion.

🚀 131K Organic / Month 👤 1,900 New User Signups / Month 🔗 DR Growth: 38 → 72

Strategy: Hyper-Specific Problem Solving

Scribe realized that broad topics like "employee onboarding" didn't convert leads. When they pivoted to targeting "SOP Templates" (Standard Operating Procedures), their sign-up rate exploded because they were solving the searcher's *entire* problem, not just a fraction of it.

"The goal isn't just to be helpful. It's to be the complete answer. If you only solve 5% of their problem, you only get 5% of their trust." — Jakub Rudnik, Head of Content
Search Volume Capture (Niche)~92%
Domain Authority (DR) GrowthDR 72 (+34 pts)

🎯 Execution Lessons

  • Journalistic Writing: Applying clear, concise news-room writing standards to technical content.
  • Batch Deployment: Publishing 70 articles at launch rather than dripping one-by-one to signal authority to Google immediately.
  • Lead Attribution: Attributing signups to specific articles allowed them to double-down on winning topics.
