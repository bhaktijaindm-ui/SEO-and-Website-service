The Journalism Approach to SEO

Jakub Rudnik, Head of Content at Scribe, brought a 'newspaper' mindset to their growth. Instead of sales-first copy, they used a journalism-based process: find the actual expert, use real data, and write with extreme clarity. But their biggest breakthrough was the Problem-Solution Match.

"How much of this problem does our tool actually solve? The higher the percentage, the more likely the content will convert." — Jakub Rudnik

The Core Strategy

01
Solving 100% of the User's Problem Instead of broad terms like "onboarding", which Scribe only solves 5% of, they doubled down on "SOP templates", where Scribe solves 100% of the manual labor.
02
The Batch Publishing Method Prior to launching,They wrote 70 high-quality articles and published them all at once on day one. Google indexed them almost immediately, cutting months off the growth timeline.

🎯 Key Takeaways

  • Be the solution, not just the information. One targeted article is worth 50 generic ones.
  • Publish in bulk: Speed up ranking velocity by giving Google consistent signals.
  • Track conversion metrics, not vanity traffic: Traffic that doesn't sign up is useless.
