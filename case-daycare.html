The £13.5M Local Strategy

When Tim Hanson took over the SEO for a premium London nursery group, they were invisible in search. Two years later, they were generating millions in revenue from parents searching for childcare. The breakthrough didn't come from massive content, but from Technical URL Architecture and Money Keywords.

"In some instances, we changed one heading and two weeks later we've gone from position 5 to 3. That doubles the clicks." — Tim Hanson

The Core Breakthroughs

01
The URL Architecture Fix Changing URLs from 'company.com/soho' to 'company.com/our-nurseries/soho'. Adding 'nurseries' to the path signaled the exact service to Google, skyrocketing rankings in 21 days.
02
Money Keyword Selection Focusing on BOFU (Bottom of Funnel) terms like 'premium daycare Soho prices' rather than broad informational terms. High conversion over high volume.
