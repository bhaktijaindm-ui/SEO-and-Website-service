The Product-Led Masterstroke

Most SaaS companies write generic guides. Typeform did the opposite: they built thousands of FREE form templates and embedded the ACTUAL product inside the SEO landing pages. When a user searches for "customer satisfaction survey template", they don't read about it—they start using it immediately.

"Relate your SEO to your product as closely as possible—and not just create boring educational content." — Jake Stainer, Ex-Typeform Head of Growth

The Strategy: Template Library Scale

01
Template-Specific Keyword Mapping They identified 1,000+ niche use-cases (HR, Event, Clinical, Feedback) and built standalone pages for each, ranking for high-intent 'template' queries.
02
Embedded Conversion Points Instead of a CTA button at the end, the template IS the content. Users start filling the form on the landing page, dramatically increasing free-trial signup rates.
