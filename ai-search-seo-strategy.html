Common Questions on AI Search

Will AI search kill traditional SEO?

No, it's evolving it. Traditional SEO techniques like technical performance and backlinks are still critical, but the way content is prioritized has shifted toward authority, proprietary data, and specific intent-matching that AI can easily categorize.

What is SGE Optimization?

Search Generative Experience (SGE) is Google's AI-driven search results page. Optimization for SGE involves structuring content so that Google's AI can easily use it as a primary source for its conversational summaries, including clear citations and "Entity-first" writing styles.

How do I track rankings in AI Search?

Traditional rank trackers are becoming less accurate for AI. We use advanced tools that monitor citations within SGE boxes and AI snapshots, giving you a 'Share of Voice' metric that is far more valuable than a simple #1 position.

Does AI content rank well in Google?

Google evaluates content based on its value (E-E-A-T), not just who wrote it. However, purely AI-generated content often fails because it lacks the "Experience" part of E-E-A-T. Our approach is 'AI-Native' meaning we use AI for research and scale, but ensure every word is humanized and expert-verified.

What is Entity Authority in SEO?

Entity authority is how 'sure' a search engine is about who your brand is and what you represent. By building out a clear, interlinked knowledge base on your site, you become a recognized 'Entity' in Google's Knowledge Graph, which is the ultimate gold standard for AI search rankings.
