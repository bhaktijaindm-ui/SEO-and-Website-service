The Tool-First Framework

Tim Soulo, CMO of Ahrefs, built their SEO strategy on a single strict rule: If a reader can't use Ahrefs to solve the problem described in the article, don't write the article. Every single piece of content they produce is a product demonstration in disguise.

"We only write about topics where our tool is part of the solution. If the reader can't use Ahrefs to accomplish something described in the article, we don't write it." — Tim Soulo
