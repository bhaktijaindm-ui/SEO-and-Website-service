How Monday.com Wrote 1,000 SEO Articles in 12 Months

Scaling organic presence as a competitive moat before their multi-billion dollar IPO.

Strategy: The Content Factory

Monday.com shifted from a heavy ad-spend model to a structured, high-velocity SEO machine. They didn't just write; they engineered a production line for high-quality technical content.

"Winning search for every relevant term was the mission. We built a system where expert writers matched the technical depth of the user base."
