Monday.com: The 1,000 Article Content Factory

Deep Dive · B2B SaaS · Content Strategy

How Monday.com Wrote 1,000 SEO Articles in 12 Months

Scaling organic presence as a competitive moat before their multi-billion dollar IPO.

📈 1,570% Traffic Growth 🎯 1.2M Monthly Visitors 📝 1,000 Articles / Year

Executive Summary

Monday.com is a project management tool that transformed from a heavy ad-spender into an SEO powerhouse. By building a high-velocity "Content Factory," they dominated thousands of keywords in the project management niche.

"The goal was simple: win the search results for every relevant term. We didn't just write; we engineered a production line for high-quality technical content."
Traffic Scale (Monthly)1,200,000+
Keyword Coverage (Top 10)~85%

The Strategy: Scale Through Systems

Unlike most companies that hire a few writers, Monday.com built a vetted recruitment pipeline. They prioritized writers with specific industry expertise (Construction, Marketing, HR) to ensure every technical article read as if it were written by a peer, not a generalist.

🎯 Technical Takeaways

  • Processes over "Rockstars": A repeatable system scales better than any individual contributor.
  • Style-Guide Rigidity: Maintaining 1,000 articles requires absolute consistency in tone and internal linking.
  • Search-Intent Matching: They focused heavily on keywords searched right before a purchase decision.
