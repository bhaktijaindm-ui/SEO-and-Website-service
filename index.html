Our Framework

The 8 Layers of Search Dominance — Our Holistic SEO System

Most agencies focus on one or two SEO factors. We built a complete 8-layer framework that covers every signal Google and AI search engines use to rank websites today. This is what makes our approach different from anyone else you'll find.

L1

SEO (Traditional Search)

Rankings, crawlability, and indexing for traditional search engines. The non-negotiable foundation of everything.

L2

GEO (Local Proximity)

Dominating local map results and "near me" search queries so nearby customers find you first — not your competitors.

L3

AEO (Answer Engine Optimization)

Winning featured snippets, People Also Ask boxes, and voice search results to capture attention at the top of the SERP.

L4

AIO (AI Search Optimization)

Getting your brand cited inside ChatGPT responses, Google SGE (Search Generative Experience), and AI Overviews.

L5

SXO (Search Experience Optimization)

Optimizing user experience, accessibility, and dwell time so visitors stay longer and Google rewards you with higher rankings.

L6

Schema (Structured Data)

Implementing rich schema markup to unlock star ratings, FAQs, prices, and other rich results directly in the SERPs.

L7

Sitemap (Strategic Architecture)

Building a logical, hierarchical site structure that helps Google discover, crawl, and index your content faster.

L8

Performance (Core Web Vitals)

Crushing Google's Core Web Vitals and PageSpeed benchmarks so your site loads instantly and ranks confidently.

How long does SEO take to show results?

SEO is a long-term strategy. Most clients see meaningful improvements in rankings and traffic within 3–6 months of consistent work. The longer you invest, the stronger and more compounding your results become.

What makes you different from other SEO companies near me?

Unlike many generic agencies, we use a proprietary 8-layer SEO framework that covers everything from technical SEO and content to AI search optimization and local rankings. You get a dedicated team, plain-English reporting, and a strategy built specifically for your business.

Do your SEO services near me include content writing?

Yes! Every plan includes SEO content creation. We write E-E-A-T optimized blogs, landing pages, and service pages that are designed to rank and convert. Higher-tier plans include more content pieces per month.

Do you offer local SEO for businesses in my area?

Absolutely. Local SEO is one of our core specialties. We optimize your Google Business Profile, manage local citations, and build geo-targeted landing pages so your business ranks at the top of "near me" searches.

How do you report on SEO progress?

We provide clear, easy-to-read monthly reports using GA4 and Google Tag Manager. You'll see keyword movements, traffic growth, backlink acquisition, and ROI data — all in plain English, never jargon.

Can I upgrade or change my plan over time?

Yes. As your business grows, your SEO needs grow too. You can upgrade your plan at any time, or speak to our team about a fully custom package built around your exact goals.

How fast will my website be?

We aim for a 90+ score on Core Web Vitals. Every site is optimized for performance, image lazy-loading, and efficient code execution.

Will the design be mobile-responsive?

Absolutely. We follow a mobile-first approach, ensuring your brand looks stunning and functions perfectly on all devices.
