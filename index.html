Our Framework

The 8 Layers of Search Dominance — Our Holistic SEO System

Most agencies focus on one or two SEO factors. We built a complete 8-layer framework that covers every signal Google and AI search engines use to rank websites today. This is what makes our approach different from anyone else you'll find.

L1

SEO (Traditional Search)

Rankings, crawlability, and indexing for traditional search engines. The non-negotiable foundation of everything.

L2

GEO (Local Proximity)

Dominating local map results and "near me" search queries so nearby customers find you first — not your competitors.

L3

AEO (Answer Engine Optimization)

Winning featured snippets, People Also Ask boxes, and voice search results to capture attention at the top of the SERP.

L4

AIO (AI Search Optimization)

Getting your brand cited inside ChatGPT responses, Google SGE (Search Generative Experience), and AI Overviews.

L5

SXO (Search Experience Optimization)

Optimizing user experience, accessibility, and dwell time so visitors stay longer and Google rewards you with higher rankings.

L6

Schema (Structured Data)

Implementing rich schema markup to unlock star ratings, FAQs, prices, and other rich results directly in the SERPs.

L7

Sitemap (Strategic Architecture)

Building a logical, hierarchical site structure that helps Google discover, crawl, and index your content faster.

L8

Performance (Core Web Vitals)

Crushing Google's Core Web Vitals and PageSpeed benchmarks so your site loads instantly and ranks confidently.
